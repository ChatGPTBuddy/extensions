Common questions

How do I install Inspire Me?

Visit the Chrome Web Store listing and click "Add to Chrome". Once installed, the extension icon appears in your browser toolbar.

What does Inspire Me do?

Toont willekeurige inspirerende quotes die je niet snel herhaald krijgt dankzij slimme tracking.

Does Inspire Me collect my data?

No. See the Privacy Policy for details.

The extension isn't working — what should I do?

  1. Make sure you're using the latest version of Chrome.
  2. Try disabling and re-enabling the extension in chrome://extensions.
  3. If the issue persists, email support with a description of what you tried.
